AI-Powered Payment Collection for Modern Finance Teams

PaygentForce connects Slack, Salesforce, Stripe, and more to automate invoice tracking and payment collection. Reduce manual work, accelerate cash flow, and keep your entire revenue workflow transparent in real time.

Ask for invoice status, trigger collections, and notify your team without leaving Slack. PaygentForce handles the heavy lifting—checking pre-mandates, coordinating approvals, and logging every update back into Salesforce.

How PaygentForce Works

Step 1 · Ask in Slack

Finance teams message PaygentForce with an invoice ID. Instantly receive account details, invoice status, and a pre-mandate check within the same conversation.

Step 2 · Collect or Close

Choose whether to collect payment immediately. If the pre-mandate is approved, PaygentForce completes the payment automatically. Otherwise, a one-click approval email is sent to the customer.

Step 3 · Notifications & Updates

Every action is captured in your #payment-agent-notification channel. Salesforce is updated in real time— invoice status, pre-mandate flags, and customer approvals stay in sync without extra work.

System Architecture

PaygentForce orchestrates secure payments across your stack using the AP2 protocol. It lives on Heroku, talks to Salesforce for data, stores state in PostgreSQL, and handles transactions through Stripe—with the A2A layer ensuring trusted communication.

  • Slack — your control center for starting every workflow.
  • AP2 protocol — secure agent-to-agent transaction standard.
  • Heroku-hosted PaygentForce — coordinates data and actions.
  • Webhook integrations — keep every system aligned in real time.
